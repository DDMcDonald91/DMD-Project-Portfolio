"Why Code?"

October 8, 2020 by Dominik McDonald

"So why code?"

Well to be honest I love the possibilities that can be created. If you apply your mind you can create anything from a simple website to apps and more. The critical thinking thats involved is something that I've grown to love even at times when I feel as though I'm struggling. I just enjoy the mental challenge that coding provides.

"What are your short term goals with coding?"

I want to gain experience working in front end web design and development. I truly do have a passion for this field and moreso the work that comes into putting together the different sites and apps we all use everyday. Being part of that and doing that for a living is something I'm looking forward to.

"Realistic Expectations"

Well realistically I expect this to be a struggle. I know theres alot I need to learn that I don't know now. I'm aware that even the skills I have now need to be worked on as well. I know there are possibly more suitible candidates on paper than myself, espically with me being self taught. However I'm open to learn everything that is offered to me to help me become a stronger programmer. I truly do want to become as knowledgable as I can be in this field.
